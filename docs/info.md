## How it works
This is a simple 4 Bit Adder.

## How to test
Connect Buttons (or Dip Switches) to the inputs.
0 to 3 are for the first number, and 4 to 7 for the second number.
Connect LEDs to the outputs.

## External hardware

8 buttons and 8 leds
